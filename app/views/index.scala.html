Everybody texts. But texting has its limitations.

There are a lot of miscommunications and insincere feelings while texting, and this is because texting doesn't let you convey any more than just text. We hope to solve this huge problem by allowing users to convey emotions and feelings in ways that texting nor face-to-face conversations has allowed us to.

Theme

TiwiTalk will detect the mood of your conversation and change your background color accordingly. You may also select themes to set the mood yourself.Your customization. Your style. Your communication.

Fonts

Being able to choose your font are the flare to your conversation. Pick something fun, flirty, classic, or serious from our many offers to put your personal touch on your conversation.

Time

Your time is precious. Let your friends know whether you want to talk or not by changing your availability setting. Placed at the top of each conversation screen for your convenience.
